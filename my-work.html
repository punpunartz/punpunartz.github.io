PunPun Short Comics

I illustrate short comics for social media involving my two main characters PunPun and Niko, telling funny stories through their interaction and any life experience they have.

People find them amusing and sometimes relatable which motivates me to keep doing what I love.

PunPun and Friends

My illustrative work is followed by PunPun, Niko, and their friends.

These two are like Tom and Jerry, treating each other like siblings with stomps and glares. But in the end, they love each other. Sometimes I draw any other thing that comes to mind.

Animation Studio

PunPunimations is the official name for my animation studio, a studio where all of my animated projects will be published under, including the adventures of PunPun and her owner Niko as they film themselves talking about any topic.

Its YouTube Channel is already in motion, so you can watch the first video already.
