Who am I?

My name is Nichole M. Ramos León and i'm a graphic designer, illustrator and animator who loves to express herself through her creations. I've always had a passion for drawing, so it has guided me to where I am today. I am a college graduate from University of Puerto Rico of Humacao with a BA concentrated in Communications with emphasis in Graphic Designs. Being a graphic designer has filled my life with amazing opportunities and I wouldn't change it for anything.

In College I had the opportunity to be part of my department's Comunication Association, AECOM (Asociación de Estudiantes de Comunicación), as Public Relations which makes me able to prepare myself for the future by illustrating and managing social media. I will add that I've worked under the supervision of one of the best professors in the department, Marcia Pacheco, and I was able to create a welcoming animation for the new members of AECOM. Such projects fill me with enthusiasm, skills, and strength when it comes to design and animation. Right now i'm a proud member at a printing shop in Caguas working as a Graphic Designer, putting my skills to the test everyday.

I aspire so many things, but one of them is to put my animations out to the world and be recognized by my talent. Having my original character PunPun come to life through my drawings and animation is my main goal. She thrives me to continue, to create comics, to enhance her story, and to make others laugh; someday she will be so big that my dream of being recognized will become a reality.
